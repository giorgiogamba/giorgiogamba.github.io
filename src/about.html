About me

I am Giorgio Gamba and I am a programmer from Turin, Italy. I studied Computer Science at the Computer Science Department of the Turin University, where I achieve a Bachelor's Degree in Human-Computer Interaction and a Master's Degree in "Virtual Reality and Multimediality". During this years I learned the Computer Science fundamentals, with a deep knowledge especially in Graphics and Rendering, Artificial Intelligence and Software Development. Now I am working as a Game Programmer at 34BigThings, a game studio in Turin, where I have been convering many fields of software developmente, from UX development to Tools creation and Gameplay features implementations. During my spare time I am a keyboard player and, DJ and electronic music events promoter and organizer.
